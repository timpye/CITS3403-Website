Peripherals

Description

Peripherals are external devices that are connected wired or wirelessly to a computer, they are considered to be essential to operate a computer as they used to input/output information from a computer, can you imagine operating your computer without a keyboard and mouse?

List of Peripherals

  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
  • Monitor
  • Printer
  • Fax machine
  • Headset
  • Speakers
  • Modem
